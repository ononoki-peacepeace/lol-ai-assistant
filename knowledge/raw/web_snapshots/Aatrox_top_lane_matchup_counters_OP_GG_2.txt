# Darius Top Lane Matchup Guide
I'm the guy who posted the Garen matchup guide earlier. Since I'm a Garen/Darius two-trick, I wanted to write one for Darius too.. My IGN is MessiOfTFT#재미뗘. If you check my match history, I haven't played Darius much lately, but he was my most played top laner before my main account got perma-banned. After playing a few games, it seems like the matchups haven't changed much, so here it is... Please feel free to give me any feedback or advice.
\*Extremely Difficult\*  Honestly, there's just no answer if the skill level is the same. Back when the Precision tree had Tenacity, you could catch him once you got Merc Treads because the stun would wear off faster, but now that the Tenacity rune is gone, it's hopeless. If Kennen just plays disciplined and doesn't get greedy, you'll never find a kill window all lane until their jungler shows up and dives you. Rush Merc Treads no matter what.  She's just great at bullying bruisers, but her roaming is so good that it feels like Darius's strength as a lane bully just disappears. Plus, when she takes Flash + Ignite/Ghost with Nimbus Cloak, she's really hard to catch. She also makes it hard to control bushes with her W. If you do manage to pull her with E, you have to move forward immediately to block her path back to her tower.  If you time your E at the same time as her Condemn, you can kill her or at least force all her summs. But if you whiff your pull and the lane is long, Vayne can pop Ghost and either force your summs or just kill you.
Anyone who brings double combat summs like Barrier, Heal, Exhaust, or Ignite is tough because Darius can't burst them in one rotation (and if I don't kill him in one go, I'm the one who dies). You can almost always kill him if the jungler comes, so if your jungler looks like they have nothing to do, spam assist pings on top.
\*Difficult\*   It's all about spacing. Both have ranges that are the same as or slightly shorter than Darius's pull, so rush Mercs or Plated Steelcaps and look for a good E or Ghost window.  About two years ago, I felt like I could win the 1v1 until the game ended, but lately, it gets really tough once she hits level 6 and gets Tier 2 boots. Her W makes it hard to close the gap and she slows you with Q. Still, Darius wins pretty much no matter what before level 6, so you have to stomp her early to build a lead in items and XP. Swiftness Boots are recommended.  His kit basically counters Darius's skills. If you use Q, he jumps in with his Q; he blocks your autos with E. Still, if you try to buffer your W when he starts spinning and use your EQ combo well, it's a winnable fight...  You absolutely have to dodge his E. Against Darius, they usually go Ignite and AP. Try your best to dodge the E and kite him well. Out of the 'Difficult' tier, I think he's the hardest for me.  You have to bully him early just like Kayle. His CC-immune passive cooldown gets shorter as he levels up, but he's still squishy in the early-to-mid game. If you can just pop his passive, you'll have plenty of chances to kill him.   They're easy if they don't take Phase Rush, but I've never seen anyone not take it against Darius.  Garen has good sustain, so he can smack the Maiden, heal up, and then go for the kill. But Darius lacks that sustain, so while you're hitting her, your own HP drops faster. You have to kill Yorick himself, but between getting hit by the ghouls and the Maiden while being trapped in his W, you'll realize why he's a hard counter. If your jungler is free, ask them to use Smite on the Maiden.
\*Skill Matchup\* but Darius has a slight edge
These two can ignore your Q or E, so it's a bit risky. Still, I think it's a skill matchup where Darius is slightly favored. Laning against these two is actually really fun.
Against Fiora, you're basically dead if you lose the Riposte mind game, but Darius won't get one-shot even if he gets stunned by it. Even if she Ripostes your Q, you still get the heal. Personally, I think Darius has the advantage here.  He's a classic counter, but I haven't seen him top since Divine Sunderer was removed.. so I'm not really sure about this one.
True, Phase Rush itself just feels like a hard counter to Darius. Even players who don't usually run it seem to pick it up as soon as they see a Darius.
He's definitely strong, but it feels like people stop falling for his tricks as you climb higher.
